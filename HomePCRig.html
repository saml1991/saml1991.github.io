Desktop build

This chronicles the changes of my home setup

2022 updates

I found some amazing floorstanding speakers on Gumtree and just about got them home! One of the tweeter domes are pressed in so may do a post repairing it. My desktop is now in the cupboard under the stairs.

The PSU bearing started making a lot of noise so I took this opportunity to replace to a modular PSU after 11 years service!

2021 updates

Ugrades!

I got a Ryzen 5 5600X, new motherboard and 32GB RAM. In my pursuit of silence I upgraded the HSF and hid the PC away in another room running a 10m HDMI cable and 2 3m USB extensions (for wireless keyboard and mouse dongles) through the loft into my silent media room.

2019 updates

The desktop and 32 inch TV had served me well for 8 years but I felt it was time for 4K. This upgrade triggered the need for a new graphics card that can utilise the latest hardware acceleration for 4k content. I went with the 1030 GPU. I also cleaned out the PC, and replaced the case fans with second hand Noctua fans. At some point this year my AV Amplifier stopped powering on. I diagnosed it as a faulty capacitor and replacing it fixed the issue.

2012 updates

Due to being in a small room for student acommodation I added a large HSF to lower computer noise and added a 2tb HDD
